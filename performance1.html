PEDRO SANCHEZ

INTO THE RING

Fascinated by animals since he was young, and particularly attracted by big cats, PEDRO SANCHEZ , son of the famous big cats tamer FELIPE SANCHEZ,
presents you his new show with his two companions and old friends ROYA the tiger and BEZOUL the lion.

A stupefying show, come and discover the talented PEDRO SANCHEZ, the man DANCING with wildcats.
You will be carried away in a wild universe in which our tamer will face the ferocious instincts of his predators. But not only...
In this brand new show, he presented us in WORLD FISRT, a choreography on furious rhythms with his two old partners, about which old and young will be filled with wonder in front of this show, where man and wildcats, make an impressive threesome..
